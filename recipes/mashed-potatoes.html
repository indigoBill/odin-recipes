Mashed Potatoes

DESCRIPTION

A warm and simple dish meant to put the mind at ease. The potato is arguably one of the most versatile foods.

It can be fried, boiled, baked, and more! The potato is a gift that keeps on giving.

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 lbs potatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Sea salt
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 4 oz cream cheese

STEPS

  1. Cut potatoes into evenly-sized chunks, and transfer them to a large stockpot full of cold water.
  2. Stir in garlic and 1 tbsp of sea salt into the water. Cook until knife inserted in the middle of a potato goes in easily with almost no resistance. Carefully drain out all of the water.
  3. While potatoes are boiling, heat the butter, milk, and an additional 2 tspns of sea salt together until the butter is just melted. Avoid boiling the milk. Set aside ntil ready to use.
  4. Used potato masher, mash the potatoes to your desired consistency.
  5. Pour butter mixture over the potatoes, and fold in with a wooden spoon until potatoes have soaked up the liquid. Repeat with the cream cheese
