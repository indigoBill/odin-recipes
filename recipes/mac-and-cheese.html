Macaroni and Cheese Pie

DESCRIPTION

A family favorite is back with a bang! Mac and cheese has never tasted so good. This dish is fit for all ages and really brings back memories of warm nights with every bite.

Let's hear it for this star-studded food superstar!

INGREDIENTS

  • 16 oz elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 2 teaspoons white pepper
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 stick of butter, cut into small pieces
  • 2 tbsp sour cream
  • 1/2 lb Velveeta, cut into small chunks
  • 8 oz shredded Colby-Jack cheese
  • 7 oz shredded Gouda cheese

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350.Bring large pot of water to boil and then salt generously. Cook macaroni al dente. Drain well and place in baking dish
  2. IN same pasta pot, whisk together whole milk, evaporated milk, eggs, salt, peppers, onion powder, and garlic powder until combined. Pour mixture over cooked macaroni in dish
  3. Add butter, sour cream, Velveeta, and half of the shredded cheese mixture. Stir to combine well and top evenly witht the remaining shredded cheese.
  4. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes until bubbly and lightly browned on top. Let rest 10 minutes or so before serving.
