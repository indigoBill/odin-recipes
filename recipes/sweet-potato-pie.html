SWEET POTATO PIE

DESCRIPTION

A sweet treat that can be paired with ice cream, whipped cream, and more. This sweet potato pie recipe will definitely have you coming back for more!

In just a few simple steps, you'll be one your way to dessert heaven!

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 whole medium sized sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 unbaked pie crust

STEPS

  1. Wrap sweet potato in foil and bake for 65 minutes at 400. Remove the skin.
  2. Break apart sweet potato in a bowl. Add butter, and mix well with mixer. Stir in sugar, milk, eggs, nutmeg, cinnamon, cloves, ginger, lemon juice, flour, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed until mixture is smooth. Pour filling into an unbaked pie crust.
  3. Bake at 350 for 55 to 60 minutes, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Pie will puff up like a souffle, and then sink down as it cools.
